# Qargo
### Regional Logistics Orchestrator — Dominican Republic Hub

> *"We own nothing. We optimize everything."*

Qargo is a logistics intermediary that helps international companies reduce costs and complexity by consolidating shipments through the Dominican Republic — a world-class regional hub connecting Europe and the Americas.

---

## What We Do

Most companies shipping between Europe and the Americas do it wrong — separate shipments to each country, fragmented routes, high costs, no visibility. Qargo redesigns that.

**The Qargo Model:**
```
Europe ⟷ Dominican Republic Hub ⟷ Caribbean + Latin America
```

**Westbound:** European brands consolidate through DR → distribute across Caribbean + LATAM
**Eastbound:** Caribbean + LATAM producers consolidate through DR → ship to Europe

We coordinate. We optimize. We earn a commission on every shipment and a percentage of the savings we generate for clients.

## Revenue Model

Qargo earns on every shipment coordinated:

| Service | How We Earn |
|---------|------------|
| Ocean & air freight coordination | Margin on negotiated rate |
| Cargo consolidation | Fee per consolidated shipment |
| Warehousing & fulfillment | % of warehousing cost |
| Customs coordination | Fixed fee per clearance |
| Regional distribution | Margin on distribution cost |
| Cargo insurance | Commission |
| Last-mile delivery (DR) | Per-delivery fee (owned service) |
| Small parcel / courier | Margin on courier partner rates |

**Plus:** % of savings generated for the client.
