# Qargo — Contact Strategy & Outreach Plan
## Spain → DR Corridor: First 90 Days
*Version 1.0 — March 2026*

---

## Overview

We have identified a $1.17B/year corridor (Spain → DR) with 100+ active Spanish companies, documented logistics pain points, and no dominant intermediary. This document outlines how we systematically work through that market.

**Goal for first 90 days:** 3 signed pilot clients, each doing at least 1 shipment through Qargo.

---

## Phase 1: Intelligence Gathering (Week 1–2)

Before sending a single pitch email, we build the full prospect database.

### Actions

**Day 1–3: Request the directories**

Email ICEX Santo Domingo:
> Subject: Solicitud de directorio de empresas españolas en RD — Qargo
>
> Estimados,
> Mi nombre es [Name], socio fundador de Qargo, una plataforma de optimización logística especializada en el corredor España-República Dominicana. Estamos en fase de lanzamiento y nos gustaría obtener el directorio actualizado de empresas españolas operando en RD. ¿Podrían facilitarnos acceso al directorio 2024?
> Muchas gracias.

Email CAMACOES:
> Subject: Presentación de Qargo — Nuevo operador logístico España-RD
>
> Estimados,
> Me dirijo a ustedes para presentar Qargo, una plataforma logística especializada en el corredor España-República Dominicana. Nos gustaría explorar la posibilidad de presentar nuestros servicios a los miembros de la Cámara. ¿Tendrían algún espacio disponible en su próxima reunión de miembros?
> Quedo a su disposición.

**Day 3–7: Build the database from public sources**

Work through these in order:
1. CESCE Exporters Ranking → exportadores.cesce.es (filter: República Dominicana)
2. DataComex → datacomex.comercio.es (filter: ES exports to DOM)
3. Cámara de España directory → camara.es
4. LinkedIn search: "export manager" + "Dominican Republic" + Spain

**Day 7–14: Qualify the list**

For each prospect, record:
- Company name
- Sector
- Estimated export volume to DR
- Key contact name + title
- Email / LinkedIn
- Current logistics provider (if known)
- Pain point hypothesis (based on sector)

Target: 50 qualified prospects before first outreach.

## Phase 2: Association-First Outreach (Week 3–4)

**Why associations first:** One meeting with ASCER reaches 200+ ceramic companies. One presentation to CAMACOES members reaches 400. This is 100x more efficient than cold email.

### Priority Association Contacts

| Association | Contact Point | Pitch |
|-------------|--------------|-------|
| CAMACOES | info@camacoes.org.do | Member meeting presentation |
| ASCER (ceramics) | ascer.es | "We specialize in ceramics logistics to DR" |
| FIAB (food & beverage) | fiab.es | "DR is Spain's #1 Caribbean wine market" |
| OEMV (wine) | oemv.es | "We handle the full wine export chain to DR" |

### Association Pitch Framework
1. Request 15 minutes at a member meeting
2. Present: "3 logistics mistakes Spanish companies make when exporting to DR — and how to fix them"
3. Offer: Free logistics audit for any member company shipping to DR
4. Collect: Contact details from interested members

## Phase 3: Direct Outreach — Tier 1 Targets (Week 4–8)

Work the Tier 1 list systematically. One company per day, personalized outreach.

### Sequence for Each Prospect

**Day 1:** LinkedIn connection request (no message yet)
**Day 3:** LinkedIn message (short, personalized)
**Day 7:** Email (if no response on LinkedIn)
**Day 14:** Follow-up email (if no response)
**Day 21:** Final follow-up + close the loop

### LinkedIn Message Template (Spanish)
```
Hola [Nombre],

Vi que [Empresa] lleva años exportando a República Dominicana — un corredor que conozco muy bien.

Estoy co-fundando Qargo, una plataforma logística especializada en este corredor. Ayudamos a empresas españolas a reducir costes y eliminar los problemas de documentación/aduanas que suelen aparecer en esta ruta.

¿Tendría 20 minutos para contarme cómo gestionáis actualmente la logística a RD? Sin compromiso — solo quiero entender si hay algo en lo que podamos ayudar.

Saludos,
[Tu nombre]
```

## Phase 4: Hotel Chains — Separate Track (Week 4–8)

Hotel chains are a different motion — they're B2B procurement, not individual sales. Treat separately.

### Approach
1. Identify the **supply chain / procurement director** at Spain HQ of each chain
2. Initial contact: LinkedIn or email to procurement director
3. Pitch: "We handle end-to-end logistics for hotel supply chains Spain → DR — F&B, amenities, linens, equipment. We consolidate across your different suppliers and deliver to your properties."
4. Key metric for them: **cost per delivery + reliability score** — they care about never running out of supplies at a resort

### Contacts to Find
- Meliá: Supply Chain Director, Palma de Mallorca HQ
- Iberostar: Procurement Director, Palma de Mallorca
- Barceló: Logistics Manager, Palma de Mallorca
- Riu: Supply Chain, Palma de Mallorca
- Catalonia: Procurement, Barcelona

## Phase 5: Pilot → Case Study → Scale (Week 8–12)

### The Pilot Offer
For first clients: offer a **1-shipment pilot at cost** (we make zero margin). The goal is:
- Build the operational track record
- Generate a real cost comparison (their old cost vs Qargo)
- Get a testimonial and case study

### The Case Study Formula
```
[Company] was spending €X per shipment Spain → DR with Y days transit
and recurring documentation problems.

With Qargo: €X-20%, Y-5 days transit, zero documentation holds.

"Quote from their logistics manager."
```

This case study becomes the sales tool for every subsequent prospect in that sector.
